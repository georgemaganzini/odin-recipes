Sweet Potato Fries

Description:

Delicious and healthy sweet potato fries! "These sweet potato fries are a great side dish that my family loves."

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes (3)
  • Olive Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic Salt
  • Sriracha
  • Honey
  1. Cut Sweet Potatoes into fry-shaped slivers, try to keep size uniform.
  2. Preheat Air-Fryer to 385.
  3. Toss sweet potatoes in Olive Oil + Salt & Pepper so that they are covered evenly.
  4. Lay out sweet potatoes on wire frame tray or air-fryer basket with tray underneath
  5. Put potatoes in oven for about 6 minutes taking them out halfway to flip.
  6. Check on fries every minute after 6 until they are ready.
  7. Mix Sriracha and Honey to use as dipping sauce.
  8. Enjoy!
